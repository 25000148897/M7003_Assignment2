# M7003_Assignment2
The right word: cognition and mediation in the case of LLMs and the writing style of psychology papers

This project investigates stylistic changes to writing in psychology papers before and after the widespread adoption of large language models (LLMs).

## Corpus

- Pre-LLM papers (n = 30) - 10 submitted here
- Post-LLM papers (n = 30) - 10 submitted here
- Declared-LLM papers (n = 10) - 5 submitted here

There were restrictions on the numbers of files that could be uploaded to GitHub.

## Methods

Texts were extracted from PDFs and analysed using Python.

Key metrics:
- Grammar errors per 1,000 words
- Mean surprisal (predictability)
- Lexical diversity (MTLD)
- Burstiness (sentence-length variability)
- Syntactic complexity
- Reference density

Figures and statistical tables are available in the `/outputs` folder.
